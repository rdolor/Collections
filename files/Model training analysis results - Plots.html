CONCLUSION¶

  • Hourly and Daily training has a tradeoff of

    • using less or more training data => less data, lower training time (less number of epochs needed)
    • use 'hour' feature or not => when hourly trained, it is better to exclude the 'hour' feature

  • Considering the training time and the metric result, it would be better to just train DAILY
